About this Website

Founded in 1996, the Baltimore Ravens have been one of the most successful teams in American Football. With a historic record of 214-169-1 (Wins-Losses-Ties), 6 Division Championships, 2 Conference Championships and 2 Super Bowl Championships, they are a force to be reckoned with. They are home to multiple Hall of Fame players, coaches and managers, which even further prove their track record in success and excellence. This website aims to showcase the team's best players, historical moments and winning cultures.
